Dataset Information

Dataset: World Bank Population, total

Indicator: SP.POP.TOTL

Data units: Countries and economies with valid World Bank region metadata

Area encoding: Larger rectangles represent larger population values.

Color encoding: Color hue represents the World Bank region, while shade intensity helps distinguish population size within the selected year.
